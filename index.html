NEWS PUBLICATIONS ABOUT

Hi! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Groningen, working with prof. dr. M. (Malvina) Nissim. Before that I worked on a Machine Translation project as a Research Assistant, collaborating with dr. Antonio Toral Ruiz and dr. Rik van Noord.

Previously, I was a PhD student at the Computational Linguistics department, University of Groningen, supervised by prof. dr. M. (Malvina) Nissim and dr. A. (Antonio) Toral Ruiz. My PhD project mainly focuses on natural language understanding and generation, especially text style transfer and creative text generation. I was born in Hainan ( 海南岛), China. I received my master's degree from China Academy of Engineering Physics in 2019 and my bachelor's degree from Central South University in 2015.

Research

My research interests lie in the broad field of natural language processing and machine learning. Currently, I am particularly interested in generative AI and reasoning generalization in large language models.

  • Generative AI
    LLM Reasoning; Multilingual/Low-resource NLP
  • Machine Learning
    Transfer Learning; Reward Learning; Efficient Learning
  • Language Generation
    Controlled Text Generation; Machine Translation; Evaluation
