NEWS PUBLICATIONS ABOUT

Hi! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Groningen, working with prof. dr. M. (Malvina) Nissim. I received my PhD from University of Groningen, supervised by prof. dr. M. (Malvina) Nissim and dr. A. (Antonio) Toral Ruiz. My PhD project mainly focuses on natural language understanding and generation, such as text style transfer. Prior to that, I received my master degree from China Academy of Engineering Physics in 2019 and my bachelor degree from Central South University in 2015. I was born in Hainan ( 海南岛 ), a lovely tropical island in the southernmost province of China.

My research interests lie in the broad field of natural language processing and machine learning. Currently, I am particularly interested in generative AI and reasoning in large language models.

  • Generative AI
    LLM Reasoning; Multilingual/Low-resource NLP
  • Machine Learning
    Transfer Learning; Reward Learning; Efficient Learning
  • Language Generation
    Controlled Text Generation; Machine Translation; Evaluation
